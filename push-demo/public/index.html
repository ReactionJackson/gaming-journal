Push Demo

A tiny PWA to test push notifications

How it works

1. Go to the Friends tab
2. Tap "Send me a push in 10s"
3. Lock your phone (press the sleep button)
4. Wait 10 seconds...
5. You'll get a notification saying "hello"!

Setup

Make sure you've added this to your Home Screen (Share > Add to Home Screen) and allowed notifications when prompted.
